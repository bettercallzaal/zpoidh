Clear the bar = you are in the split. There is no judging, no ranking, no "best." Everyone who genuinely shipped shares the pot equally.


WHAT COUNTS (so the floor is honest, not a loophole)

+ It has to be yours - your build, your hands, your screen in the photo.
+ It has to be from this build month - not something you shipped last spring and re-posted.
+ It has to be linkable - a URL anyone can open: live app, repo, mint page, published post, watchable video.
+ One real build per person in the split. Ship five things if you want - you are still one builder, one slice. This is participation, not volume farming.
+ Effort floor, not a quality gate. We are not scoring craft here. But an empty repo, a screenshot of someone else's work, or a "coming soon" page does not clear the bar.
