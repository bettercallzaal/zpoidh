## What worked

- Episode-tied source content (Ep 17 already had an audience watching)
- Clear submission proof (X URL of the clip)
- 10 unique editors brought first-timer POIDH submitters into the ZAO album
- OPEN bounty type let the pot stack publicly

## What broke (lessons folded into R2 + R3)

- No upper time limit on video - submissions varied wildly in length
- Vague "your favorite clip" creative brief - winner pick felt arbitrary because rubric was implicit
- No per-submission scorecard page - submitters had no insight into how they were judged
- Cycle took ~6 weeks from cast to winner accepted (slow judging)
