Audio rule (every BCZ POIDH bounty)

No random library music or ambient pads under spoken dialog. If you want non-silence, use the official campaign promo MP3 (e.g. zabal-gamez-promo.mp3), original source-episode audio, or one clear instrumental that does not compete with dialog. Layered melodic music over spoken dialog = automatic floor fail.
